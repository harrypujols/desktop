about

Harry Pujols (1973- ): Born in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic. He was exposed to English and R-rated movies at a tender age thanks to cable TV. He made his first comic book at age 8. He wrote his first program in Basic in a Tandy TRS-80 at age 10. Somehow he thought his mix of words, pictures and technology was appropiate to try advertising school. In 1996 he designed the first version of the site you're looking at now. In his free time, he competes in endurance sports. Harry currently lives in New York City and has no cats.
