Dynamic site prepared in 2010 for New York local business Hamlet's Vintage.

Objective: Create a custom site supported by a full-featured Content Management System so the site's administrator can update its content.

Technology used: Wordpress, JavaScript, JQuery, XHTML 1.0 transitional.

Software: TextWrangler, Firefox with Firebug plugin.

I also helped the store set its social media accounts in Facebook and Twitter.
