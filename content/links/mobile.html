Ongoing project which started in 2011 as a complement to the full site.

Objective: Have a leaner, portable site for devices using the QR code printed in my resumes, business cards, stationery and other identity items. .

Technology used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Jquery.

Software used: TextWrangler, iOS Simulator.

Chances are you won't see the current page on a phone. It's supposed to take you directly to the mobile site.
